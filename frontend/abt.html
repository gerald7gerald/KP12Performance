[ WHO I AM ]

Who I Am

I'm Kyle Perera, the founder of KP12 Performance. My background is in kinesiology — I earned my B.S. from Cal State Fullerton and I'm finishing my Master's in Applied Exercise Science at Concordia University Chicago. Outside of KP12, I teach kinesiology and head coach the swim and water polo programs at St. John Bosco High School, and I spent years coaching competitive swimmers at the Los Cerritos YMCA. KP12 Performance is where all of that comes together — real science, real coaching experience, and a genuine belief that performance is built through consistency, not shortcuts.
